Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer gluten-free options?
We know dietary needs matter! Give us a call at (864) 308-1226 and we'll be glad to go over our current gluten-free options with you.
Do you have vegetarian options?
You bet! Try our Harvest veggie pizza, a Build Your Own loaded with your favorite veggies, or a fresh House Salad.
Can I order online for pickup or delivery?
Absolutely. Tap any Order Online button to order through Toast for pickup or delivery — it goes straight to our kitchen.
What sizes do your pizzas come in?
Two sizes: a 10-inch (perfect personal size) and a shareable 16-inch. Pick one of our specialty pies or Build Your Own in either size.
Do you accept credit and debit cards?
Yes, we accept major cards. A small 3% processing fee is added to card payments to help cover the cost.
Can I download the menu?
Sure can! View our full menu — opens in a new tab with both pages, and there's a Download button there if you'd like to save it.
